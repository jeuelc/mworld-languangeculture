If you were making a manga, what sort of setting would you choose?

Manga can be set in worlds you know, like school or sports clubs, and worlds you dream about, like outer space, ancient Greece and Rome, samurai Japan, and alternative dimensions.

When people talk about the 'world' of a manga, this can mean the look and feel, or the style of a manga, as well as the time and place that the story happens.

Some manga are incredibly detailed and some have a more simplistic style. Some are set in the past - depicting real events or imagined - and others are set in the present. Some even imagine the future. While many manga are set in Japan, some are set in other parts of the world, and some are set in completely imaginary realms!

A well-planned world provides plenty of ideas for manga artists to write stories.
