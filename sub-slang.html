Slang

Most people take their language for granted, but think about it the fact that you can speak something as complicated as a human language is probably your most amazing intellectual achievement!

Your language enables you to convey ideas, facts, opinions and emotions. It also gives you a social life. So language provides both the building blocks for communicating and the mortar for sticking people together.

The English language is central to what makes you tick and so is its slang. It's there in the thick of it, playing crucial roles in the hard work of communicating and connecting.

Whatever people might think about slang (and most people have opinions!) it deserves to be studied.
