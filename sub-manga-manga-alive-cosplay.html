'Cosplay' is short for 'costume play'. Fans dress up as their favourite characters from a work of fiction - mostly these are manga and anime characters. But there are also video game characters, comic book heroes, and movie characters. Cosplay outfits have even had an influence on Japanese street fashion.

If you dress up for a fancy dress party or for something like Halloween, it doesn't matter if your costume is accurate or not. But if you're taking part in cosplay, it matters! At conventions, there are even contests where participants are awarded points for accuracy!

Serious cosplayers spend many hours researching and constructing their outfits. Sometimes, manga costumes appear to defy gravity - such as giant floating bows, or capes that always appear to flow. These are very tricky to make and require a lot of creativity!
