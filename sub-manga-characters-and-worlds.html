Characters and Worlds

Why are manga so popular? Two big reasons are manga's strong characters and worlds. People enjoy manga stories so much that they are retold over and over, and characters jump out of manga and into other media!

First, each manga story will be serialized (published in parts) in magazines. Then, if it's popular, it will be republished as a standalone book or book series. If it becomes even more popular, it will be turned into an anime! Then if the anime is successful, merchandise that uses the show's characters will be made! There are games, dolls, costumes, magazines, novels, cinematic comics, illustrations, posters, TV dramas, movies, and music based on manga. All of these forms explore the worlds and characters of the original manga in new ways, giving fans the chance to enjoy their favourite stories all over again.
