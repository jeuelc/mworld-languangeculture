A diverse nation

Nations define us. They give us passports to travel the world, a team to follow in the Olympic Games or a World Cup, and a sense of belonging, but what makes a nation a nation?

We tend to believe that all citizens of a nation share likes and dislikes, speak the same language and see themselves as part of a unified community. Also, we tend to think that countries were always there. However, nations dont just exist; they're built over the years; they've changed and are still changing.

Come and see how Spain as a nation came to exist. You'll discover how a country's borders can come about by battles won and lost and how nations can be made up of quite different regions and peoples.
