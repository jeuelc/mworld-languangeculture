Castilian, Catalan and Galician are all languages that developed from Latin, the language of the Romans. So they share many characteristics, as they also do with French, Portuguese and Italian.

Basque is unrelated to any other language. Some people think it is the original language spoken by the earliest Europeans. It doesn't look or sound like the other languages spoken in Spain or anywhere else in the world. It's a mystery!
