Other Worlds

If a nation consists of people who share a language, geography and history, then is Spain a nation? It has multiple languages and its regions have a strong sense of the historical and cultural difference which makes up their Basque, Catalan or Galician identity. Some people from these areas claim they are each their own nation, not Spain. Is Spain, then, a 'composite' nation? A nation of different nations?

There is no single way of being a nation. Whats your country like?

Let's see how some other nations see themselves. But lets not forget our common humanity. While things might look a bit different on the surface, we share many things with citizens of other countries.
