Making Manga

Have you ever thought about making your own manga? How would you start?

If you want to create your own manga, the first steps are to invent the plot, including your characters, and the world they will inhabit. Next, you need to set out the story in panels, and begin to sketch the characters, backgrounds, and speech bubbles. These are drawn over with ink, then shades, tones, and corrections are added, and finally, the words are added to the speech bubbles.

Manga artists use special ways to express characters' feelings, movement, the passing of time, and the atmosphere of an environment.

Tap on the panels in the manga to explore how the different elements work together to convey action and emotion, and try making your own manga!
