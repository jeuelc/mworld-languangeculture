Many slang expressions are created by distorting the shape of words.

Often combining with other processes, this kind of word makeover is at the heart of a lot of playful slang.

Refashioned swearwords offer some of the best examples of this kind of verbal play.

'Drat!' and 'Rats!' are playful remodellings of what was once a truly awful curse 'God rot!' It meant 'May God rot you!' or anyone else! The process is still responsible for some spectacularly transformed swearwords you might hear people use today. Fiddle-faddle! and Shucks! sound like much ruder exclamations but they are a lot safer to use around parents and teachers!
