Fans love to go to manga and anime conventions where they can meet other fans and enjoy their favourite manga!

One of the longest-running conventions, Comiket (short for 'Comic Market') began in 1975. Just over 600 people went, and it was held mostly for fans to buy and sell their own homemade comics called 'dojinshi'. Now, Comiket is held twice a year and about half a million people go each time!

At conventions there are often discussions with creators, manga drawing workshops, anime screenings, costume (cosplay) contests, art shows, and social activities. Of course, many commercially made books and character goods are on sale as well! But fans still produce a lot of their own artwork and even their own character goods and manga magazines.
