Why Use Slang?

Slanguage exists for a lot of reasons. Here are some of the most important ones:

To define the gang sharing the same slang is about unity and belonging.

To show familiarity slang marks activities, events and objects that are routine.

To stand out slang wants to amuse and perhaps even shock outsiders with novelty.

To have fun people love playing about with language.

To safeguard privacy or secrecy slang can be about hiding information.
