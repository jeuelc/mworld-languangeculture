A lot of slang expressions are created by blending two (or occasionally more) existing words. Here are two:

fantabulous (from fantastic + fabulous)

stease (from style + ease)

Dozens of new blended words are appearing all the time and some of them can be very clever. Try to figure out which words produced in the factory have the following meanings:

gorgeous

attractive, but empty-headed man

the disease of being too rich

collapsible bottle

publication for enthusiasts

the practice of decorating overhead wires with shoes

a state of anxiety brought on by not having mobile phone contact
