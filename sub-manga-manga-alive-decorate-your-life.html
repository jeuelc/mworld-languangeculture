Do you own things with manga characters on them? What's the most interesting character merchandise you've ever seen?

Manga/anime characters are often turned into merchandise called 'character goods'. These could be jewellery, lunch boxes, toilet seat covers, slippers, food, drinks, and more. You can even buy tissues with your favourite characters printed on them!

One of the biggest manufacturers of character goods is Sanrio. Their most famous character is Hello Kitty. Although Hello Kitty is not originally from a manga, she is drawn in a manga style and now there are heaps of Hello Kitty manga and anime. Hello Kitty has appeared on thousands of products, including toasters, toothbrushes, toilet paper, and wedding dresses. Some dedicated fans decorate their entire home with Hello Kitty!

Another character who appears on many goods is Doraemon. Doraemon is a robotic cat who originally appeared in the manga by Fujiko Fujio. There are Doraemon key-rings, clocks, T-shirts, iPhone cases, nail clippers, shower caps, car mirrors, suitcases, soup mugs and USB chargers!
