Cute and Cool Characters

 

Lots of western comics are about heroes who have superpowers. In manga, the main character may or may not have super powers. There are lots of manga about kids just like you.

When you create a manga character, there are two important things to keep in mind. First is your character's background. You need to decide the character's name, age, gender, and so on. What is their personal history? What is their family situation? Who are their friends? These details make your character more believable.

Second is your character's design. You need to draw their features. Are they young or old? What clothing do they wear? What do they normally carry?

Tap on the character to explore the visual conventions used in character design!
