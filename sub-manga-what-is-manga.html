What is Manga?

 
click here click here

Manga are Japanese comics. Naruto, One Piece, Dragon Ball, and Sailor Moon are some of the famous ones. A lot of anime, or Japanese cartoons, were originally based on manga.

Every week, 1.6 billion (that's 1,600,000,000!) manga magazines and books are printed in Japan! That's enough for 10 volumes of manga for every person in Japan to read each week! Manga in Japan are sold in bookstores, convenience stores, train stations, and second-hand shops.

Why are manga so popular?

One reason is that manga are aimed at all age groups and interests. There are manga about action and adventure, science fiction, romance, robots, magical quests, as well as everyday topics such as cooking, board games, sports, office work there are even manga about school!
