A lot of professional manga artists still draw their comics by hand. They use art supplies like pens, ink, mechanical pencils, rulers, brush pens, and correction fluid.

Most of the early sketching is done using a light blue lead in the mechanical pencil. Because manga is printed in monochrome (black printing on white or light-coloured paper), the blue colour isn't picked up in the printing process. So if you forget to erase some of your sketching, it doesn't show in the final manga. But you can also use a normal grey lead pencil and an eraser!

After the sketching is complete, ink is used to draw over the final pictures. Even professional manga artists sometimes make mistakes! This is what the correction fluid is for. But it is important to wait for the ink to dry first, or you'll end up with a mess!
