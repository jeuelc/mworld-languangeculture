Nations have borders. Borders mark where citizens of one country have the right to live, vote in elections, pay taxes, are schooled in a particular way, etc. Nations watch their borders, allowing or stopping entry by people who arent citizens. Usually to cross from one country to another you need to show your passport or an identity card.

Spain is in south-western Europe on the Iberian Peninsula. The Pyrenees mountain range is its border with France in the north-east and on the west is Portugal. To the south is the Mediterranean Sea and to the north is the Atlantic Ocean. It wasnt always like this. For centuries part of southern France belonged to Spain, but the Spaniards were forced to give it up after losing a war in 1659. Spain also consists of the Balearic Islands in the Mediterranean and the Canary Islands, off the coast of west Africa over 2,000km from mainland Spain!
