Over the years people have described slang in lots of ways, some flattering but many very unflattering. Slang is often misunderstood.

People often connect it with swearing, but this is wrong. Sure, slang can be rude, and you'll be seeing examples later, but it's not necessarily connected to taboos in the same way that swearing is.

Many people also wrongly identify slang with the way teenagers speak, as something most people eventually grow out of. Of course, teenagers use slang, but then so do adults. Most importantly though, they never use each other's slang!

As you will discover, slang has been with us for 'donkey's years' (in other words, a very long time) and is evolving every day!
