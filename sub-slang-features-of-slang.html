Features of Slang

So, you want to create some slang? Far out! It's happening around the world right now and can be a lot of fun. Before you enter the Slang Factory, here are some basics you need to dig:

1. Slang thrives in situations that are informal. Think 'playground' not 'science class'!

2. Slang is typically heard and not written, unless of course the internet or texting is involved.

3. Slang mostly involves words and phrases, but pronunciation and grammar can also be slangy.

4. Slang expressions are lively and provocative. They grab your attention!
