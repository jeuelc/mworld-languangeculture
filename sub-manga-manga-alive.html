Manga Alive!

Is manga part of your life? Look around you. Sometimes it is, even when you don't realise.

Art forms like manga can develop a life of their own.

Manga has become much more than just a form of comic book. Manga drawing style and characters even influence the way people dress and decorate their homes.

Every year, hundreds of thousands of people around the world visit manga conventions. There, you can dress up as your favourite manga or anime character, buy manga character goods, or manga which has been made by fans.

Discover how manga plays an important part in the lives of young people around the world!
