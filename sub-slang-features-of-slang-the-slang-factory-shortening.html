When we're in familiar territory, we take shortcuts! A lot can be taken for granted when you're chatting with someone you know well in a relaxed environment. So you can afford to drop consonants and vowels here and there while still being understood. It's not surprising then that a lot of slang expressions are words that have been shortened in some way.

Texting and internet chat have been triggers for a huge number of new words to be created and the reason they are taken up so rapidly.

Perhaps in a matter of hours, new slang creations will be seen around the world! It has become almost impossible for printed dictionaries to keep up with the changes. New expressions appear, old expressions alter their appearance, their meaning and their grammatical behaviour and it's all happening at breakneck speed!
